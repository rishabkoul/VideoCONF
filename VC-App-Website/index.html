Introduction

We are a team of computer science engineers who are very passionate to deliver. We are currently in the ideation face of our project. We have already concluded a survey of sample size on 2500 people which include students, teachers, professional and business people. We carried out a survey in which we studied the number of users using different video conferencing applications like Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, WebEx, and other leading apps in the market, and found out the PROS and CONS faced by them on each of these applications. On the basis of this survey we have devised a solution for all the problems, and created an idea to bring together all the features loved by the users by implementing a vast array of technology stack to give the best user experience.

OUR FEATURES:

Better Security

AI integrated improved security and end-to-end encryption

More Social

A solution supporting more than 1500 participant limit

24/7 HelpDesk

Assistance at anytime with a dedicated chatbot feature


Design-

  • User-friendly environment.
  • Impressive UI / UX.
  • Will consist of different functions for different categories of people to make their work more efficient.
  • Each category is provided with some category specific feature. Whereas if other categories want any feature from other categories then it will be added to that category for free or as a premium feature depending on the choice.

Quality-

  • 1500+ Number of people in a single Free Video call.
  • No limit on video calling timing.
  • Premium features with impressive costs.
  • More secure with the use of AI.
  • Face and fingerprint feature.

Additional Features-

  • Has a virtual assistant for help 24/7 (Help Desk).
  • A fast and responsive feedback system.
  • Availability of Chatbot for any queries.
  • Integrated board(Jam Board).
